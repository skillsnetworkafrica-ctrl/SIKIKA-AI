WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT

can hear.

Lecturer draws this
Visually impaired student hears

"Flowchart with 3 elements. A rectangle labeled 'Client' at the top center. Arrow from Client pointing to 'Server' at the bottom-left. Arrow from Server pointing to an ellipse labeled 'Database' on the right."

Not a screen reader. A diagram intelligence engine.

Identifies shapes, arrows, text labels. Computes spatial positions. Traces connections. Infers diagram type. Speaks it aloud via text-to-speech.
