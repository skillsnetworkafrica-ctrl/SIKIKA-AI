The Problem

University lectures are delivered through sound and sight. A deaf student can't access sound. A blind student can't access sight. Both paid full tuition. Kenya has just ~200 certified sign language interpreters for 54 million people — that's 1 interpreter per 270,000 citizens (KNCHR, 2022). Hiring one costs $25–$60/hr, costing universities $50K–$300K/yr. Even then, 20–30% of content is lost (Marschark et al., 2015). You cannot hire enough humans. The supply does not exist.
