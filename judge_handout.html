The Problem

University lectures are delivered through sound and sight. A deaf student can't access sound. A visually impaired student can't access sight. Both paid full tuition. Kenya has just ~200 certified sign language interpreters for 54 million people — 1 per 270,000 citizens (KNCHR, 2022), costing $25–$60/hr with 20–30% content lost (Marschark, 2015). The supply does not exist.
